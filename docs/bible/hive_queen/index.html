Hive queen

hive_queen
Epic

Apex predator found in power generation buildings where massive electromagnetic fields created the conditions for a colonial organism. The queen is the command node for a network of smaller drones that she spawns during combat. She rarely engages directly until her drone screen is destroyed, at which point she becomes extremely aggressive. Territorial — she will not leave her building.

SLOP's ecological survey classifies this entity as a 'senior unauthorized biological occupant with delegation authority.' She has established a management hierarchy that SLOP grudgingly respects. Her quarterly output of subsidiary organisms exceeds the pre-collapse HR department's hiring rate by 400%. SLOP has considered offering her a consulting position.
