Game Design Document // 2026-02-27

A post-apocalyptic base-building survival game where you reclaim real buildings by restoring their mechanical systems. Explore and clear hostile structures in first-person, switch to isometric view to design Satisfactory-style automation networks connecting your growing territory. Defend your factory base from escalating fauna waves. Play solo or drop-in/drop-out co-op.

Home Base Factory

Full Satisfactory-Style Building Inside Your Base

Your home base is one node among many -- it can smelt, process, and assemble just like any building. But inside, you get full freeform factory building.

  • Foundations, machines, conveyors, splitters, mergers on a snap grid
  • Receives intermediate products from the network (ingots, components, circuits)
  • Also processes raw materials if you want to do it locally
  • Final assembly for weapons, tools, armor, turrets, advanced MEP parts
  • Research / tech tree unlocks new machines and recipes
  • You decide: centralize production at the hub, or distribute across the network

Threat Meter

Global Threat Level

Rises when you connect buildings, increase throughput, or expand territory. Higher threat means tougher fauna, multi-direction assaults, and boss spawns. Baseline waves prevent turtling.

Critical

Power Dependency

  • Turrets and spotlights need electricity
  • Lose power = defenses go dark
  • Generators are high-value targets
  • Route power strategically under strain
