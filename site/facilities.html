Home base operations

Your factory

How factory building works

Your home base is where Satisfactory meets survival. Lay foundation tiles on the terrain. Everything snaps to the grid. Place smelters, assemblers, conveyor belts, splitters, mergers, storage bins. Route materials between machines. Stack foundations for multi-story layouts. The factory you design IS your base — your production layout becomes your defense perimeter.

Foundations

Build the grid

Lay foundation tiles on terrain. Stack them with spacing for multi-story. Zoop-style batch placement for fast layout. Everything snaps to foundations.

Machines

Production stations

Smelters, assemblers, workbenches, fabricators. Each takes inputs and produces outputs. Place them on foundations, connect with conveyors.

Logistics

Conveyors and storage

Belt conveyors carry items between machines. Splitters divide flow. Mergers combine it. Storage bins buffer materials. Route everything.

Defenses

Walls and turrets

Walls enclose your factory. Turrets snap to walls and platforms. Power them from generators. Your production layout shapes your defensive geometry.

Inter-facility logistics

The production network

The big picture

Every reclaimed building becomes a production node in a distributed supply chain. Buildings process raw materials into intermediate goods. Those ship to your home base or to other buildings for higher-tier production. The whole thing is a factory that spans the map. You manage it from the isometric overworld — set recipes, assign output destinations, monitor throughput. No conveyor spaghetti between buildings — supply lines are abstract connections.

Power plant

Output: electricity

Required by other buildings. Lose power and everything stops.

Foundry

Output: iron / copper / steel ingots

Smelts raw scrap into usable metal. The first link in every production chain.

Warehouse

Output: sorted raw salvage

Sorts and distributes raw materials to specialized buildings.

Machine shop

Output: mechanical components

Turns ingots into gears, pistons, bearings. Mid-tier production.

Water treatment

Output: clean water

Required by some production processes and the hospital.

Electronics lab

Output: circuit boards

Copper ingots + components = circuits. High-tier production.

Hospital

Output: medical supplies

Chemicals + water = meds. Keeps your crew operational.

Office building

Output: research points

Generates the currency for the tech tree. Unlocks new recipes and machines.
