Dept. 03 // Safety — Unauthorized biological occupant status report

The fauna

All sectors remain within manageable parameters. SLOP has classified the biological entities currently occupying company facilities as "temporary wildlife intrusions." Restoration personnel are reminded that engagement with unauthorized occupants is covered under standard workplace hazard policy. Life insurance claims may be delayed.

What SLOP isn't telling you

The complex is crawling with mutated creatures. The type of industry that ran in each building shaped what evolved in the aftermath. Chemical plants bred toxic spore creatures. Heavy manufacturing produced biomechanical hybrids with machine parts fused into their bodies. Warehouses are infested with coordinated pack hunters. Power plants are the territory of apex predators.

Biological occupant classification

Fauna by biome

Each building type spawned its own ecosystem. The industry that ran there shaped the mutations.

Chemical processing

Spore creatures

Slow-moving but toxic. Fungal growths cover their bodies and spread to nearby surfaces, creating contamination zones. Spore clouds corrode equipment and damage players over time. Keep your distance.

Heavy manufacturing

Biomechanical hybrids

Machine parts fused into organic tissue over generations. Hydraulic limbs, gear joints, steel plating grown into the hide. Fast, armored, aggressive. The factory made them.

Warehouse / Logistics

Pack hunters

Small, numerous, coordinated. Bioluminescent markings pulse in sync across the pack — they move as one organism. They nested in the shelving and shipping containers. Getting surrounded is the danger.

Power generation

Apex predators

Massive, territorial, drawn to energy output. Electrical discharge crackles across their bodies. Fewer in number but each is a boss-tier encounter. They guard the most valuable buildings.

Perimeter integrity systems

Defense

Active

Auto-turrets

Snap to foundations and walls. Require power and ammo. Primary ranged defense. Multiple types as you unlock the tech tree.

Passive

Spike walls and wire

Damage on contact, slow the advance. Funnel creatures into kill zones. Cheap, disposable, effective.

Infrastructure

Power dependency

Turrets and spotlights need electricity from generators. Lose power, lose defenses. Generators are your most critical infrastructure.

One-use

Landmines

Crafted at the fabricator. Area denial. One detonation per mine. Place them where the waves will come.

SLOP would like to reassure all personnel that the current defensive infrastructure is adequate for projected threat levels. SLOP's projections are based on models that have not been updated since before the collapse. Confidence level: high.
